About Us

For over 15 years, Pink Pearl Senior Care has supported families by providing holistic, insured, and trusted in-home care for seniors. Our team is dedicated to creating a serene, safe, and nurturing environment tailored to each client’s needs—helping seniors live independently with dignity and joy.

Our Services

  • Personalized companionship
  • Light meal preparation
  • Errands & shopping assistance
  • Wellness and mobility support
  • Respite care for families
  • Medication reminders
  • Daily routine assistance
  • Social & recreational activities
